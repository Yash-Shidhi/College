Group Schemes

Corporate Health Insurance

Description: Comprehensive health insurance plans tailored for corporate organizations.

Features:

  • Coverage for employees and their families
  • Customizable plans based on company size and budget
  • Access to network hospitals and healthcare providers
  • Wellness programs and health screenings

Group Life Insurance

Description: Life insurance coverage offered to groups such as employees, members of an organization, or associations.

Features:

  • Financial protection for beneficiaries in case of the insured's death
  • Employer-sponsored or association-sponsored plans
  • Flexible coverage options and benefit amounts
  • Optional add-ons such as disability coverage

Student Health Insurance

Description: Affordable health insurance plans specifically designed for students.

Features:

  • Comprehensive coverage for medical expenses
  • Access to a network of doctors and hospitals near your college
  • Prescription drug coverage
  • Mental health support and counseling services
  • 24/7 helpline for medical emergencies

Group Study Abroad Insurance

Description: Insurance coverage for students studying abroad in a group.

Features:

  • Medical coverage for accidents and illnesses while abroad
  • Emergency medical evacuation and repatriation
  • Travel assistance services
  • Coverage for lost or stolen belongings
  • 24/7 multilingual customer support
